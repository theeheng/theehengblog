Bonnard Review February

Bonnard

Visitor fell in love with his intimate domestic scenes and vibrant landscapes, captured through his unconventional but masterful use of colour. The paper rhapsodised that the exhibition was 'the sumptous harvest of Bonnard long devotion to what can only be called the Sublime... Bonnard's individuality has been dispersed among his thoundsands of imitators. But this retrospective reasserts his position as a great modern master'.

Twenty-one years later, the forthcoming Pieere Bonnard will revisit this beloved painter.
